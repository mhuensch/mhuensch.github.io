Inspiration

Here is a running list of all the projects and people I've learned from and taken inspiration from.  They are all fantastic, entertaining, and educational.

Brent Amberger

Brent's channel (Goobertoon Hobbies) is a fantastic resource if you're looking to answer tough questions about hobbying.  Through testing and SCIENCE!, Brent finds the answer to tough questions like "What paint stripper works best?" or "Which varnish will protect my miniatures?"

Marco Frisoni

Marco is crazy passionate about miniatures and painting and it really shows on his Youtube channel.  If you're looking to get deeper into some of the theory behind painting as an art, Marco is a great resource.

Dana Howl

Tired of the same old minis?  Looking for some inspiration that doesn't come directly from the box art?  Dana's channel is a great place to start!

OnTableTop

Confused about how to play Kingdom Death: Monster?  Looking to learn how to play by example?  Join On Table Top and Darker Days Radio as they play through 14 years and see how the game is played.

Guy Perchard

If you're looking for some instruction that is simple and straight to the point, Guy's channel (Midwinter Minis) is for you.  At the time of this writing, his content is almost entirely focused on 40K miniatures, but it's still a great resource.

Mark Sorastro

If you've looked at any of my painted mini's, you'll instantly recognize that I've cribbed a lot from Mark's painting and modeling.  Sorastro's Painting is an amazing channel and Mark is an amazing artist.

Vince Venturella

Vince's "Hobby Cheating" videos were some of the first videos I watched when learning how to paint miniatures.  Vince is an incredible artist and has an amazing ability to make difficult processes seem simple.  Wether your an experienced painter or a total noob, there's a lot you can learn from Vince Venturella.

Scott Walter

Scott (aka Miniac) is another amazing painter producing videos on Youtube.  Not only will he teach you interesting things about painting your miniatures, he does quite a few videos with practical comparisons of techniques, mediums, additives, etc.

James Wappel

James is an extraordinary artist.  If you're looking for in-depth, real time, insights to take your painting to the next level, check out his twitch stream where he regularly paints amazing miniatures from start to finish.

Zatcaskagoon Miniatures

Zatcaskagoon Miniatures is a great resource for Grim Dark painting tutorials.  His free content on Youtube is worth a look.
